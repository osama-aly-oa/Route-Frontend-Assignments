Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about buying, selling, and investing in real estate.

You're typically ready to buy when you have a steady income, a good credit score (usually 620+), and enough savings for a down payment and closing costs. We recommend speaking with one of our mortgage partners to get a clear picture of your budget.

Generally, spring and early summer are considered the best times to sell. However, market conditions vary by location. Our agents can provide a specific market analysis for your neighborhood to determine the optimal listing time.

Your offer should depend on the home's condition, time on market, and comparable sales in the area. Our agents will help you craft a competitive offer that protects your interests while being attractive to sellers.

Closing costs are fees paid at the end of a real estate transaction, typically ranging from 2-5% of the loan amount. Both buyers and sellers have closing costs, but specific items like title insurance or transfer taxes can be negotiated.
